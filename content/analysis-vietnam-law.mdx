This article summarizes some information about a large dataset related to Vietnamese legal documents, extracted from the website [Thư viện Pháp luật](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/).

Data updated to June 2023. Includes 318,517 Vietnamese legal documents. Document attributes:

- Document name
- Document number
- Document type
- Document field
- Signer
- Place of issue
- Issuance date
- Effective date
- Gazette date
- Gazette number
- Status of validity
- Links between documents
